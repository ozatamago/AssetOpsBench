### Thought Process and Reasoning
To generate rules that help distinguish meaningful alerts from spurious ones, we need to analyze the available data and understand the context of the alerts. The problem involves IoT data, asset details, and potentially failure modes. We can start by identifying the relevant assets and their sensor data. Then, we can analyze the time-series data to identify patterns or anomalies that could be related to meaningful or spurious alerts. Finally, we can use the insights gained to generate rules for filtering alerts.
